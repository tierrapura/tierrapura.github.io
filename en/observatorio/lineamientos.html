Tierra Pura Foundation Guidelines

Strategic lines

The TierraPura Foundation has a series of guidelines for its structure, mission and vision

1
Promoting Nature-Based Solutions (NBS)

NBS can be described as those actions that rely on ecosystems to address problems such as climate change, food safety or disaster risk.

2
Promoting socio-economic impact 

Our aim is to benefit the communities where projects are based through education and training in an equal opportunities’ environment.

3
Ensuring the feasibility of timely project implementation

Regulatory compliance, together with collaboration between the various participants and technical, financial and risk management must be justified throughout the duration of the project, which should have an exit strategy based on a coherent and feasible business model.

4
Ensuring that investment generates additionality

The projects should generate added value leading to a paradigm shift; the necessary initial funding thus contributes to overall national progress.

Categories

Projects will be classified according to the following categories, although any one project lying within a main category may be also be associated with several sub-categories:

Climate Change
Mitigation and Adaptation
Biodiversity
Conservation and maintenance
Desertification

Sectors

Natural ecosystems
Forests, scrubland, natural and mountain grasslands, wetlands and aquatic systems
Agriculture and livestock
Regenerative agriculture and forestry-agricultural-livestock systems
